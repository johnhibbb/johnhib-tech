# Sphere

**360° reframing plugin for DaVinci Resolve**

A native plugin that brings 360 video editing into the professional NLE workflow — no context switching, no compressed intermediates, no separate apps.

## The Problem
360 creators are forced out of their NLE (Resolve, FCPX, Premiere) to reframe footage in proprietary apps (DJI Studio, Insta360 Studio), then back in to finish the edit. Reframing is a creative editorial decision — it belongs in the timeline.

## The Solution
A DaVinci Resolve plugin that:
- Imports equirectangular 360 footage directly onto the timeline
- Provides a native spherical viewport preview
- Enables virtual camera keyframing inside the timeline
- Outputs ProRes without a compressed intermediate
