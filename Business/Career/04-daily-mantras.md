# Three Daily Mantras

*Created 2026-02-23 by María*

---

These aren't affirmations. They're anchors — designed to pull you back into the frame we're building when imposter syndrome, urgency, or noise tries to pull you out.

---

## 1. "I didn't arrive here by accident."

A decade of work. Retail floors, creator studios, executive rooms, AI product teams. $100M in revenue. YouTube features that shipped to millions. A consulting practice inside one of the most scrutinized companies in the world.

You didn't stumble into AI. You walked a path that most "AI people" never had — one that taught you what technology adoption actually looks like from the human side. That path *is* the qualification.

**When to use it:** When you feel underqualified. When the job posting says "CS degree preferred." When someone with a Stanford pedigree makes you second-guess yourself.

---

## 2. "I build the proof."

You are not a commentator. You are not a resume. You are someone who ships — agents, workflows, prototypes, frameworks, films. Every opinion you hold is backed by something you made with your hands.

The NDA covers three years of work. It does not cover your mind. What you build in public is the evidence of what you've always done in private.

**When to use it:** When the NDA feels like a cage. When you're stuck in planning mode. When you need to remind yourself that the portfolio is the path forward.

---

## 3. "Present. Grounded."

This is already yours — it showed up in your journaling, your aesthetic choices, your values. It means: stay in the reality of this moment. Not the anxiety of what hasn't happened yet. Not the story about what you should have done differently.

The strategy is built. The work is in motion. Your job today is to do *today's* work — not to solve the next twelve months in your head.

**When to use it:** When you spiral. When the timeline feels impossible. When you're awake at 2 AM running scenarios. Come back to the ground.

---

*Read these in the morning before you open LinkedIn. Read them again when you need to.*
