### How to Approach Boutiques

Unlike big firms with formal application portals, boutiques hire through:

1. **Direct LinkedIn outreach to founders** — message them with a 3-line pitch: your background, what you bring, what interests you about their firm
2. **"Join our community" portals** — Brainpool and others use network models where you join their expert pool and get matched to projects
3. **Warm introductions** — if you know anyone in the consulting world who's tracking these firms
4. **Demonstrating capability** — a portfolio or case study showing how you'd approach an AI strategy engagement carries more weight than a resume at these firms

### Why Boutiques Matter for You

- **Faster hiring cycles** (weeks, not months)
- **Value hybrid backgrounds** — they need people who can do consulting AND understand AI, not just one or the other
- **More direct client impact** — you're not a cog in a 500-person engagement team
- **Equity/upside potential** — early employees at funded AI consultancies have startup-like upside
- **Bridge to your own practice** — if you eventually want to build your own AI consulting practice, boutique experience is the fastest path
