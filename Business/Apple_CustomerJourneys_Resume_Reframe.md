## What This Role Actually Is

Apple's "Customer Journeys and Marketing" team sits within Marcom and is responsible for producing the marketing content that moves customers through Apple's ecosystem — purchase → onboarding → services adoption → loyalty. Think: the emails after you buy an iPhone, the retail experience when you pick it up, the campaigns that push Apple One, AppleCare, iCloud, Apple Music to existing customers.

This is **not** the big campaign team. That's Integrated Producer, Marcom LA (billboard campaigns, product launches, TV spots). Customer Journeys is about lifecycle marketing — the ongoing, always-on content that sustains the relationship between Apple and its customers.

The Marketing Producer on this team typically:
- Manages production of multi-touchpoint campaign assets (email, push notification, web, retail, in-app)
- Works closely with CRM, product marketing, retail, and creative to produce content at scale
- Owns timelines, briefs, asset delivery, and stakeholder alignment
- Understands customer behavior data well enough to inform content sequencing and messaging strategy

**Why this matters for John:** This is closer to his actual work than the Marcom LA role. He has been studying how creators move through a product, what they need at each stage, and building the content and tools to support that journey — for three years at YouTube.
